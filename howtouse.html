Step by Step Guide

1

Apne account mein minimum **Rs 50** deposit karein.

2

Server 1 ya Server 4 select karein.

3

Apni pasand ki **Country** aur **Service** (WhatsApp/Google etc) select karein.

4

**Buy Number** par click karein aur number ko copy karke apni app mein lagayein.

5

Wapis site par aakar **Refresh** dabayein, aapka OTP 5-10 seconds mein mil jayega.
